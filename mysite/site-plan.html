Content

Home page

Welcome to my website! I'm Dallin Hale, a 29-year-old web developer that transitioned from serving as a police officer to thriving in the tech industry. I currently specialize in developing interactive maps for a space reservation software company, but I also design web elements, create useful plugins and scripts, and manage and manipulate databases. I have 4 years experience as a full-time graphic designer, and during that time I also learned SQL, Python, Javascript, and a bit of Typescript.

Explore my work to see how I can bring creativity and precision to your products.
